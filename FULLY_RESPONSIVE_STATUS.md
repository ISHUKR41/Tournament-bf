### Section Headings:
```tsx
className="text-lg sm:text-xl 
           font-bold text-orange-500 
           mb-3 sm:mb-4"
```

### Form Grids:
```tsx
className="grid grid-cols-1 
           md:grid-cols-2 
           gap-3 sm:gap-4"
```

### Labels:
```tsx
className="block text-white 
           mb-2

```

### Containers:
```tsx
className="container mx-auto

           py-8 sm:py-12"
```
